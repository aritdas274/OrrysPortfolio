THE THINGS I DO

Graphic Design

Graphic design is the art of using visuals—like typography, colors, shapes, and images—to communicate messages, solve problems, and evoke emotions for specific audiences, blending creativity with strategic communication for both print and digital media, from logos and websites to advertisements. It's about making information clear, guiding attention, and building brand identity through intentional visual choices that connect businesses with people.

Motion graphics are animated graphic design elements, like text, shapes, and logos, brought to life with movement, timing, and sound to communicate information effectively in multimedia projects, distinct from character-focused animation by emphasizing abstract visuals, as seen in movie titles, ads, and explainer videos.
